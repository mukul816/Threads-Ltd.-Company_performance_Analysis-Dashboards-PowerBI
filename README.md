# Threads Ltd. Company Performance Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive business performance analysis of Threads Ltd. using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard is designed to help management understand company performance across sales, profit, products, retailers, franchises, and key business indicators.

The report transforms raw business data into interactive visualizations and KPIs that support better strategic decision-making. The analysis focuses on identifying high-performing products, retailer contributions, franchise purchasing patterns, revenue trends, and profitability.

---

# Objectives of the Project

* Analyze overall company sales and profitability.
* Compare product performance across categories.
* Evaluate retailer contribution to revenue generation.
* Examine franchise purchasing behavior.
* Monitor KPIs and business growth trends.
* Provide insights for improving operational and financial performance.

# Major Conclusions from the Analysis

Based on the dashboard analysis, the following conclusions can be drawn:

1. **Revenue Growth Opportunities**
   The company shows strong revenue potential, but certain products and retailers contribute significantly more than others.

2. **Product Performance Variation**
   Some products consistently outperform others in sales and profitability, indicating areas where the company should focus marketing and inventory efforts.

3. **Retailer Contribution is Uneven**
   A small number of retailers contribute a major share of revenue, highlighting the importance of maintaining strong retailer relationships.

4. **Franchise Purchasing Trends**
   Franchise purchase behavior varies considerably, suggesting opportunities for targeted franchise support and promotional strategies.

5. **Profitability Monitoring is Essential**
   Revenue growth does not always guarantee higher profits. Monitoring both revenue and profit together is necessary for sustainable business growth.

6. **KPIs Improve Decision-Making**
   Centralized KPIs help management quickly identify business performance trends and operational issues.

7. **Data Visualization Enhances Business Understanding**
   Interactive Power BI dashboards simplify complex business data and improve reporting efficiency.
